Frequently Asked Questions

How much homeowners insurance do I need?

Your coverage should be enough to rebuild your home at current construction costs, plus coverage for personal belongings and liability protection.

What factors affect auto insurance rates?

Rates are based on your driving record, age, vehicle type, location, credit score, and coverage levels you choose.

Do I need renters insurance?

Yes, renters insurance protects your personal belongings and provides liability coverage that your landlord's insurance doesn't cover.

What is umbrella insurance?

Umbrella insurance provides additional liability coverage beyond your home and auto policy limits, typically starting at $1 million.
